How to Use This Tool

This app builds a daily activity record from the entries you log throughout the day. It is not a CRM. It is a quick capture tool that turns into a formatted snapshot at the end of the day.

When to log each event

  • After Call — immediately after a Catalant, GLG, or other expert call ends.
  • After Bid — immediately after submitting a bid or RFP response on any platform.
  • After Proposal — immediately after sending a proposal to a prospect.
  • End of Day — once, at the end of the day. Enter revenue, pipeline value, pipeline opportunity count, and rate metrics from your pipeline dashboard (App 03).
  • Manual — anytime. Use for anything that does not fit the other four categories, or to add a note to today's record.

Why log in real time

Logging at the moment an event happens takes seconds and removes the end of day reconstruction work. The End of Day entry then only needs the metrics that are not captured automatically: revenue, pipeline value, and rates.

Generating the snapshot

Click Generate Snapshot at the end of the day. It compiles every entry logged today into one formatted record: bids submitted, calls done, proposals sent, time logged, platforms worked, plus the End of Day metrics if you logged one. Save it to your computer or copy it to paste into Claude for the GSD skill.

Where the data lives

Entries are stored in this browser only. They are not synced anywhere. Generate and save your snapshot before closing the browser if you want a permanent record, then use Clear Today's Log to start fresh the next day.
