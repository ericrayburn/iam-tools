App 05 Runbook — When and How to Use This Tool

What this app does

It reads live data from App 02 (Screener), App 03 (Pipeline), and App 06 (Insights) and combines those numbers with four manually entered metrics. It outputs a formatted GSD Weekly Momentum block you paste directly into Claude at the start of every Monday session.

When to run it

Every Monday morning, before you open Claude. Run it once per week. It takes under two minutes. Do not run it mid-week. The snapshot captures the prior week's activity and tells Claude where you stand entering the new week.

Step-by-step

1
Open this app first Before you open Claude or any other tool. The Live Pipeline Feed at the top reads from App 02 and App 03 automatically. No action needed on your part for those numbers.
2
Check the Live Pipeline Feed Verify the numbers look right. If App 03 shows 0 active bids but you have bids in the pipeline, open App 03 first and confirm your data is saved, then come back. The feed reads live from browser storage.
3
Enter your four numbers Bids Submitted — total bids you submitted in the prior week. Check App 03 for bids with status Submitted or Interview added in the past 7 days.
Calls Done — expert calls or discovery calls you completed. Check GLG, Catalant, or calendar.
Revenue ($) — any revenue received or invoiced in the prior week. Check your invoicing records.
Pipeline Value ($) — estimated total contract value of all active bids. Multiply your rate by estimated engagement hours for each active bid and sum them.
4
Click Generate Snapshot The formatted block appears on the right. It matches the Weekly Momentum format the GSD skill expects. Review it. If a number looks wrong, correct the field and regenerate.
5
Click Copy to Clipboard Open Claude Code. Start a new message. Paste the block. Add any context you want Claude to know, then send. Claude will read the snapshot and use it to orient the GSD session.
6
Your data saves automatically The four fields save to browser storage as you type. When you return next Monday, the prior week's numbers are still there for reference. Click Clear to reset for the new week's entry.

Where each number comes from

  • Bids Submitted — App 03 Bid Pipeline. Count bids you moved to Submitted or Interview in the past 7 days. The feed shows the running total; you enter only last week's submissions.
  • Calls Done — your own count. Check your calendar for GLG, Catalant, or direct client calls completed Monday through Sunday.
  • Revenue ($) — any payment received or invoice sent in the prior week. This is cash activity, not pipeline estimate.
  • Pipeline Value ($) — total potential value of all active bids. Example: 3 active bids at $400/hr, each 80 hours estimated = $96,000 pipeline.

What the Live Pipeline Feed shows

  • Active Bids — all bids in Draft, Submitted, or Interview status in App 03 right now.
  • Won (Lifetime) — total bids marked Won in App 03 since you started tracking.
  • Screened This Week — bid postings run through App 02 Screener in the past 7 days.
  • GO Rate — percentage of screened bids that received a GO verdict in App 02 this week.

If the feed shows no data

The feed reads from browser localStorage. It only works in the same browser where you use App 02 and App 03. If you use different browsers or devices, the feed shows zeros. Enter the numbers manually from your records. The snapshot still generates correctly.

Cadence reminder

Run this tool every Monday morning before your first Claude session. Do not skip weeks. A missing week breaks the momentum trend. If you missed a week, run it with the best numbers you can recall. A rough snapshot is better than no snapshot.
